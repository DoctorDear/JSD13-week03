How it Works

Renting professional camera gear has never been so seamless. Our step-by-step process keeps it simple.

1

Choose Rental Dates & Gear

Select your start and end dates in the date banner. The catalog dynamically hides any gear currently out on hire. Add your desired camera bodies, lenses, and accessories to your cart.

2

Identity Upload & Checkout

Go to checkout and upload a clear photo of your ID Card for verification. Click "Confirm & Book". No online payment is processed at this stage.

3

Admin Verification & Quote

A shop admin will review your ID card and contact you by phone to confirm pickup details and quote the final security deposit (fully refundable upon return).

4

Pickup at Shop & Enjoy

Visit the shop on your start date. Present your physical ID, pay the rental fee + security deposit, inspect your gear, and take it with you. Bring it back when done to collect your full deposit!

Store Policies

  • Pickup & Return Hours: Monday to Sunday, 09:00 AM - 08:00 PM.
  • Late Return Fees: Late returns will be charged at 1.5x the standard daily rate for each day past due.
  • Damage Liability: Renters are responsible for any scratches, drops, or water damage. A portion of the security deposit will be withheld if damage is found.
